About Rowan Partnerships Online

Rowan University and Rowan College of South Jersey (RCSJ) have partnered to offer students fully online degree programs at a fraction of the original cost. Upon completion of the program, students will have earned their 2-year RCSJ degree and their 4-year Rowan University degree in their respective fields of study.

A Partnership Like No Other

What happens when one of the nation's top universities and New Jersey's #1 community college join forces? Rowan Partnerships Online - that's what. Be delighted with top-notch instructors, state-of-the-art education, and all at a price that will put a smile on your face.

Start your education with faculty from the Best Community College in NJ and end your education at one of the nation's Top Universities. Rowan University and RCSJ are reimagining the world of online education. The future of online education is here; the question is: are you?
