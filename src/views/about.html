About Rowan Online

Rowan University and Rowan College of South Jersey (RCSJ) have partnered to offer students fully online degree programs at a fraction of the original cost. Upon completion of the program, students will have earned their 2-year RCSJ degree and their 4-year Rowan University degree in their respective fields of study.

Saving You Money from the Start

Student enrolled in Rowan Online get one locked-in price for all four years. Never worry about tuition increases or unexpected payments again!

What happens when one of the nation's top universities and New Jersey's #1 community college join forces? Rowan Online- that's what. Be delighted with top notch instructors, state-of-the-art education, and all at a price that will put a smile on your face.
