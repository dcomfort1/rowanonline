Bachelor of Science in Human Resource Management

In an economic world in which competitive advantage is increasingly based on knowledge and creativity, effective Human Resources functions are crucial for organizations that want to improve their productivity. The Rowan Online Human Resources Management requirements prepare graduates for the HR profession using a blend of classroom, extra-, and co-curricular experiences, such as internships and project-based learning assignments. Our program is grounded in the values of workplace equity and respect and recognition for contributions of the diverse American workforce.

Human resource management consists of planned organizational activities designed to improve employee efficiency and equity, such as staffing, compensation, and training.

Potential Careers

  • Human Resource Managers
  • Hiring Managers
  • Business Administrators
  • Benefits Specialists

Courses and Program Guide

All Rowan Online programs have been designed to be delivered in an accelerated format. These courses are designed to help you get the information you need at a pace you'll love.

Accelerating Your Learning

Rowan Online uses a 7-week format for courses as opposed to a traditional 15 week format. Rather than taking five or six courses all at the same time, take two or three and go further in-depth and get the education you deserve.
