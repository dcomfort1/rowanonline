What is Rowan Online?

Rowan University and Rowan College of South Jersey (RCSJ) have partnered to offer students several fully-online degree programs at a fraction of the cost. Upon completion of the program, students will have earned their 2-year RCSJ degree and their 4-year Rowan University degree in their fields of study.
