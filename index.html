What is Rowan Online?

Rowan University and Rowan College of South Jersey (RCSJ) have partnered to offer students several fully online degree programs at a fraction of the cost. Upon completion of the program, students will have earned their 2-year RCSJ degree and their 4-year Rowan University degree in their respective fields of study.

Apply Now

Academic Programs
All programs are taught online by RCSJ faculty during the first two years and Rowan University faculty during the final two years of the program. All students must maintain a minimum 2.0 GPA.

Choose from the following fully-online degree programs:
  • Business Administration
  • Computing and Informatics
  • HR Management
  • Psychology

Admissions
Please review the following Admissions information below:
  • Only true Fall Freshmen will be admitted to this 4-year online program
  • Students must apply at least 2-weeks prior to the first day of the RCSJ Fall semester
  • All students must have a computer that meets minimum requirements
  • This is an open enrollment program with RCSJ placement requirements
  • This program offers dual acceptance to RCSJ and Rowan University

Financial Aid / Costs
Please review the Financial Aid information below:
  • Students must indicate RCSJ on their FAFSA form (Federal School Code: 006901) when entering the program
  • Students may apply for financial aid and scholarships initially through RCSJ
  • There are no deposit requirements when entering into this program
  • Students may not be able to participate in on-campus activities, join student clubs, nor use certain onsite resources while admitted to the online program
